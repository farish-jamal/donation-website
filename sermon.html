How to get closer to Allah

Drawing closer to Allah requires sincere worship, regular prayer, recitation of the Quran, remembrance of Allah (Dhikr), and following the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Importance of Hajj in Islam

Hajj is one of the five pillars of Islam and a sacred pilgrimage to Mecca that every able Muslim must perform at least once in their lifetime. It symbolizes unity, equality, and submission to Allah.

Importance of "Piller" of Islam

The Five Pillars of Islam form the foundation of our faith and practice. Understanding and implementing these pillars - Shahada, Salah, Zakat, Sawm, and Hajj - strengthens our relationship with Allah.
