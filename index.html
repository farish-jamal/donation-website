How to get closer to Allah

Draw closer to Allah through sincere prayer, regular Quran recitation, dhikr, seeking forgiveness, performing good deeds, helping others, maintaining patience during trials, making dua, following the Sunnah, and constantly remembering Him in all aspects of daily life with gratitude and devotion.

13 Nov 2023 Admin
Importance of Hajj in Islam

Hajj is the fifth pillar of Islam, obligatory once for able Muslims. This sacred pilgrimage to Makkah purifies the soul, erases sins, unites the Ummah in devotion, and strengthens faith through submission to Allah's commands, following Prophet Ibrahim's (AS) legacy.

13 Nov 2023 Admin
Importance of “Piller” of Islam

The Five Pillars of Islam - Shahada, Salah, Zakat, Sawm, and Hajj - form the foundation of Muslim faith and practice. They establish complete submission to Allah, unite the global Ummah, purify the soul, and guide believers toward righteousness and eternal success.
