About All India Sabile Hussain trust

Allah Help Those Who Help Themselves

All India Sabile Hussain Trust is a welfare, charitable, national, educational and preaching organization whose existence is to serve the people, education and learning, advocacy and preaching, reforming society, protection of rights, brotherhood, peace and love, prevention of evil and oppression. In view of Bob, Inqlab Afarin Awaz and other similar welfare works, the All India Sabile Hussain Trust was established on 13/February 2016. And, thank God, it was officially registered on 25/February 2023. Thus, its founders are Muhammad Sajid and Muhammad Aarif Qadri, but a formal committee was formed and the officials were elected, of which Muhammad Aarif Raza Qadri was elected as the president.

Our Objectives and Mission

We envision a society where humanity, love, justice, and unity are the highest values. The vision of Sabile Hussain Trust is a world where no one sleeps hungry, no child is deprived of education, and no person in need is left without support. We strive to spread the message of service, compassion, and peace beyond the boundaries of religion, caste, or language. Our goal is to build an India where every heart is filled with empathy, every hand reaches out to help, and every individual feels the pain of others as their own. Sabile Hussain Trust — Serving Humanity, Our Mission.

Meet Our Organizer

Aarif Raza Qadri

President

The purpose of Sabile Hussain Trust is not just to serve, but to spread love, unity, and harmony through the service of humanity. We firmly believe that the world becomes a better place when human beings care for one another — regardless of religion, caste, or background. Hunger, poverty, illness, and the lack of education have no religion. Likewise, serving humanity is not limited to any single faith or community — it is our shared moral duty. At Sabile Hussain Trust, we work with the conviction that “Service to humanity is the greatest form of worship.” Our mission is to build a society where love, tolerance, and brotherhood are our true identity. We invite everyone to join us — with prayers, support, and compassion — so that together, we may light up our nation with the spirit of humanity and kindness. May Almighty Allah bless us all with sincerity, compassion, and the strength to continue serving mankind. Ameen.

MD SAJID

Founder

This Trust was not established merely to provide aid, but to bring hearts closer, to nurture compassion, and to awaken a sense of kindness within society. We believe that true faith and true humanity both shine when a person stands for others in need. Today, Sabile Hussain Trust has become a symbol of a mission that transcends religion — a mission built purely on the foundation of humanity, harmony, and hope. Our dream is to see an India where every heart beats with love, every hand reaches out to help, and every soul becomes a light for others. May Almighty Allah bless us with sincerity, patience, and the strength to continue this journey of service and compassion. Ameen.
